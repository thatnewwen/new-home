coFOUNDER

SUBTITLE

A publication and community focused on covering films from Asia and the Asian diaspora. Our goal is to shift the cultural conversation of Asian cinema to recenter Asian voices in Western discourse, apply film critique through a radical lens, and broaden the definition of Asian film canon as defined by the general public. In addition to our magazine, we also host a biweekly Asian film society, and recently planned a film festival!

coEDITOR + WRITER

Editorial

Our online magazine features longform writing on Asian films—with a focus on emerging and independent filmmakers. I serve as co-editor and writer, contributing film criticism, interviews, and festival coverage.

coCORDINATOR

Southeast Asian Short Film Festival

We created Chicago's first Southeast Asian film fest! We collaborated with DePaul University to bring over 23 short films from Southeast Asia for 3 days of film, food, and programming. I supported efforts on funding, outreach, and marketing.

FACILITATOR

Lost in Translation

We host a bi-weekly Asian film society where we watch an Asian film, and follow it up with a discussion. Recently, we've started hosting screenings online as well. We're going one year strong and counting!
